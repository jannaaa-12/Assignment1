About the Film

My name is Jana Mohsen. I am majoring in BOS and minoring in Interactive Media. I’m a senior who often walks to the convenience store during class breaks, and that routine inspired this video.

I wanted it to feel like a perspective video—what I actually see as I walk—so the filming is intentionally casual and not polished. That choice reflects how the moment feels to me, not a staged version of it.

Walking outside helps me reconnect with the space around me. I usually shuffle my playlist and let random songs carry me so I don’t waste time deciding. That spontaneous soundtrack and the short walk are the reset I need before the second half of class.

Jana Mohsen — Assignment 1, Comms Lab
