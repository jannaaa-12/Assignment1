About the Film

This video shows a student’s-eye perspective during a 15-minute break—stepping out of class to reset, breathe, and power up for the second half. The filming choice is intentional—first-person framing and simple cuts—to represent how the campus actually looks and feels from the student’s point of view.

Jana Mohsen — Assignment 1, Comms Lab
