About the Film

My name is Jana Mohsen. I am majoring in BOS and minoring in Interactive Media. I am a senior who often walks to the convenience store during class breaks, and this routine inspired the video.

I wanted it to feel like a perspective video, showing what I actually see as I walk. That’s why the filming is intentionally casual and not polished, because it reflects how I personally experience the moment.

Walking outside helps me reconnect with nature. On the way, I usually shuffle my playlist and let random songs play. This habit avoids wasting time deciding what to listen to, while also adding a spontaneous mood to my walk. These details are what I wanted the film to capture — a reset before the second half of class.

Jana Mohsen — Assignment 1, Comms Lab
